🎶 Choir Ministry Commitment Form

"Worshiping Through Song & Unity"

📜 Ministry Overview

The Choir Ministry is dedicated to worshiping God through live ministration, singing, and dancing, fostering a spirit of unity, encouragement, and praise within the congregation. Our goal is to enhance the worship experience and promote true worship, only for the glory and honor of God.

📌 Ministry Policies

  • 🎤 Attendance: Members must attend all scheduled rehearsals and services unless excused by leadership.
  • ⏳ Punctuality: Arrive 15 minutes before rehearsals and ministrations.
  • 🤝 Respect: Show kindness to all choir members and leadership.
  • 🎼 Commitment: A commitment to the choir means dedication to worship services.
  • 📅 Participation: Engage in rehearsals, ministrations, and special events.
  • 👕 Ministry Attire: Wear the attire specified by the Attire Committee.
  • 🔒 Confidentiality: Keep personal or sensitive ministry matters private.
  • 🚨 Dressing Code: No ministration in trousers or short skirts (ladies), tight trousers, T-shirts, open shoes, or unkempt hair (gents).
  • ⚠️ Probation: Misconduct (secularism, sexual immorality, inconsistency) may lead to probation or disqualification.

🎵 Ministry Activities

  • 📆 Weekly Rehearsals: Preparing for Sunday services and special worship weekends.
  • 🤝 Team Fellowship: Strengthening unity through team-building activities.
  • 🎼 Workshops & Training: Improving vocal and musical skills through learning sessions.
  • 🙏 Spiritual Growth: Engaging in prayers and scripture study for spiritual nourishment.
